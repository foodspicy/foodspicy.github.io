Food Spicy Spl. Thali

Indulge in our Food Spicy Spl. Thali at just ₹399, a symphony of bold flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Savor the perfect blend of spices with our curated selection:
Mix Veg: A medley of fresh vegetables cooked to perfection, bursting with spicy goodness.
